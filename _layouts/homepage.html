Computationally, world peace isn’t a hard challenge.
☀️

A simple information-sharing DeGroot style society, having explored this planet, would share information until they converged onto a single shared framework of belief, upheld by a collective shared knowledge base.
☀️
This was the dream of the old (2000s) internet. For a moment, we believed that all problems were just a lack of information.
